(1) Make button
   Make a new puzzle. This is same as selecting 'Make' in the main screen.



(2) Puzzle's State
   There are 3 states in a puzzle; Playing, Making and Solved. Depending on the state, the features of the puzzle screen varies a little.



(3) Slide menu
   Swiping an item to the left, you will see a menu slide in.

    * Delete
      Delete the puzzle. Before deleting it, a confirmation dialog appears.

    * Copy
      Copy the puzzle. The copy will be added to the bottom of the list.

   To hide the slide menu, swipe the item to the right.
