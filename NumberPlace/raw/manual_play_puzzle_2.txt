(2) Undo button.
   Undo one operation.


(3) Options menu
   The actions you can do is as following.

    * New puzzle
      Generate a new puzzle and play it. The current puzzle will be discarded.

    * Save
      Save the current puzzle. The saved puzzle can be accessed via 'Collection' in the main screen.

    * Restart
      Reset the current puzzle and play from the beginning.

    * Clear memo
      Clear all memorandums.

    * Find answer
      Find the answer of the current puzzle by puzzle-solver program.


(4) Play time
   Shows time from the start of playing the current puzzle.


(5) Fixed digit
   You can't fill in the grey-colored square.
