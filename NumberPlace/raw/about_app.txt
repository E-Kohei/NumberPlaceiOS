Dive into the deep world of Number-Place!
This application provides you with several utilities:
Using this app, you can

    - play large number of puzzles of 5 levels which are created randomly by puzzle-maker program,

    - make a puzzle manually and check whether the puzzle is solvable,

    - save any puzzles you're playing or making and resume playing or making the puzzles later,

    - and find the answer of 'any' puzzle quickly by puzzle-solver program!!
